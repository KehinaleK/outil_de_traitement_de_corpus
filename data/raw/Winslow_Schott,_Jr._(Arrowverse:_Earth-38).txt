Winslow "Winn" Schott, Jr. was  the son of the original Toyman and a tech expert who worked alongside Kara Danvers (Supergirl) at CatCo Worldwide Media who served as one of her allies, helping her develop her costume and aiding her in her adventures as Supergirl.[6] Winn moved into the 31st century after the defeat of the Worldkillers and joined the Legion of Superheroes. Initially he used the code name Computer Lad but later he started using the code name Toyman.
