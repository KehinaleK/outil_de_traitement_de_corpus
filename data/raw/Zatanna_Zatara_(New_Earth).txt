Zatanna Zatara, usually known by just her first name, is a powerful half Homo Magi. She is the daughter of John Zatara, and like him casts spells by saying what she wants backward. Zatanna is a direct descendant of the artist and magician Leonardo da Vinci, and is related to Nostradamus, Alessandro Cagliostro, the noted alchemists Nicholas Flamel and Evan Fulcanelli and Lord Arion of Atlantis.[5] Her cousin, Zachary Zatara, is also a magician.
