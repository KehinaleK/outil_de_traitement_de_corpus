Princess Diana of Themyscira, also known as Wonder Woman, is a founding member of the Justice League of America and one of the strongest female superheroes on the planet. She is a princess of the Amazons of Paradise Island, sculpted from clay by Queen Hippolyta and given life by the Gods of Olympus. She is a champion of love, truth and peace, and uses her god-given powers to bring these values to Man's World.
