Freddy Freeman was an average teenager and fan of Captain Marvel until he was paralyzed after an encounter with Captain Nazi. He would later find himself empowered by his best friend and secret idol, Billy Batson, as Captain Marvel, Jr., later shortened to CM3.  Eventually, he would advance to become the new magical champion of Earth under the name Captain Marvel. Freddy has prominently served as a member of the Marvel Family and the Teen Titans.
