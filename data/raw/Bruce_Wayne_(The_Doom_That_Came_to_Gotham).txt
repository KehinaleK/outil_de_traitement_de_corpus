Batman (real name Bruce Wayne) is the main vigilante operating in 1920s Gotham City. After witnessing the murder of his parents at the hands of a "mugger" as a child, Bruce waged a war on crime in Gotham. In his secret identity, he is a billionaire industrialist and notorious playboy. He fought the city's crime problems as well as Ra's al Ghul and the Iog-Sotha entity.
