Stephanie Brown is the daughter of the Cluemaster, one of Gotham City third-rate villains. To ruin his schemes, she became the vigilante Spoiler. She eventually grew into becoming the third Batgirl. Despite a long history with Robin (Tim Drake), During his retirement, she briefly claimed his position and became Batman's partner Robin, although she was fired for disobeying orders. Her relationship with Batman is less straightforward.
