Eddie Thawne was a CCPD detective transferred from Keystone City, who became the partner of the detective Joe West and the fiancé of Iris West. The most famous of his descendants is Eobard Thawne, the super-villain Reverse-Flash. Years after his death, Eddie was resurrected as "Dr. Malcolm Gilmore" by the Negative Speed Force to become its new avatar: Cobalt Blue.
